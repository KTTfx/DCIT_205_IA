Entry Requirements

Welcome to the Computer Science Department at Universtiy of Ghana! We are committed to providing our students with a rigorous and engaging educational experience that will prepare them for successful careers in the ever-evolving tech industry.

To be eligible for admission to our undergraduate programs in computer science, you must meet the following minimum requirements:

  • A high school diploma or equivalent
  • A minimum GPA of 3.0 in math and science courses
  • Strong English language proficiency

In addition to these minimum requirements, we also consider factors such as your extracurricular activities, letters of recommendation, and personal statement.

If you are interested in applying to our graduate programs, please visit our graduate program website for more information.

Finding Requirements

To find specific requirements for your desired computer science program, please refer to the program catalog.

You can also contact our admissions office at admissions@ug.edu or (555) 555-5555 for further assistance.

We encourage you to apply early to ensure your spot in our competitive computer science programs.
