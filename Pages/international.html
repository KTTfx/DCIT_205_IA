International Students

Welcome to the Computer Science Department at University of Ghana! We are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive environment for international students, fostering their academic success and personal growth within our vibrant learning community.

Whether you are considering pursuing a degree in computer science or seeking opportunities to enhance your skills and knowledge, our department is here to guide you through the process and help you achieve your goals.

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission to our undergraduate or graduate programs in computer science as an international student, you must meet the following minimum requirements:

  • A strong academic record from your previous institution
  • Proficiency in English language
  • Completion of the required application documents

For more detailed information on admission requirements, please visit our International Admissions page.

Financial Aid

We understand that the financial costs of pursuing higher education can be a concern for international students. Our university offers a variety of financial aid options to help you achieve your academic aspirations.

To learn more about financial aid opportunities, please visit our Financial Aid page.

Student Support Services

Our department is dedicated to providing comprehensive student support services to our international students, ensuring a smooth transition into the university life and a successful academic journey.

We offer a wide range of services, including:

  • International student orientation and advising
  • English language support and tutoring
  • Cultural and social events
  • Career counseling and job search assistance

For more information on student support services, please visit our International Student Support page.

Visa Information

International students are required to obtain the appropriate visa to study in the United States. The type of visa required will depend on your nationality and program of study.

For detailed information on visa requirements and procedures, please visit the U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) website.
