Enhancing Machine Learning Performance with Neural Architecture Search

Machine learning(ML) has revolutionized various industries, from self-driving cars to medical diagnosis. However, designing high-performing ML models can be a challenging and time-consuming process. This is where neural architecture search (NAS) comes in.

NAS is a powerful technique that automates the design of neural networks, the building blocks of ML models. By automatically searching through a vast space of possible architectures, NAS can identify the best architecture for a given task, often outperforming manually designed models.
