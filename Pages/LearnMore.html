Computer Science Department

Featured Program: Computer Science

Our Computer Science program provides a comprehensive foundation in the principles and techniques of computer science, preparing students for a wide range of careers in the ever-evolving tech industry.

As a Computer Science major, you will gain a deep understanding of:

  • Programming languages and software development
  • Data structures and algorithms
  • Computer architecture and operating systems
  • Networking and communication
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • Cybersecurity

With this knowledge and hands-on experience, you will be well-prepared for careers in software engineering, web development, data science, cybersecurity, and many other exciting fields.
